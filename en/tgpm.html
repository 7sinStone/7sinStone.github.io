Traffic Generation and Performance measurement

Index page

Description

It is a module integrated into the "Shinken" monitoring tool that allows the generation of network traffic and performance measurement based on open source tools: IPERF3 and D-ITG.

For this the overall architecture of the project contains:
A module integrated in Shiken which must be installed side supervisor machine. It orchestrates the whole process of the performance test.
Through it, we define the machines that will generate traffic and the machines that will be the receivers, and for each flow to generate, we define the different parameters:

protocol: specifies the protocol used by the sending machine when generating traffic. The protocol can be either TCP or UDP. In the case of TCP, traffic flows are Unicast, whereas for UDP, the administrator must be able to choose between multicast and unicast,
packet size: the size of the packets can be fixed, as it can follow a model specific stochastic,
Inter-frame time: the time delay between the arrival of two successive frames may.As in the case of packet size, be either constant or defined by a stochastic model,
sampling interval: delay between two successive measurements of a metric,
transmission time: duration of traffic generation,
transmission time: duration of traffic generation,
delay before the start of the traffic generation: allows to calculate the date of the beginning of the generation of a given stream.

A daemon that can act as a generator or a traffic receiver. This daemon must be installed on the supervised machine side.
